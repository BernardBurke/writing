# Ident — Project Context
*Last updated: [date]*

## What this is
A speculative fiction manuscript. V1 complete (stream of consciousness draft).
V2 in planning. Open-sourced because any Goodness and Wisdom that comes
from this work belongs to everyone.

## The story in one breath
Near-future. Identity has been bureaucratised into a global system (WhoComm).
The story asks: what happens to the self when identity becomes infrastructure?
And beneath that: what *is* the self at all?

## Characters
- **Greg** — technologist, WhoComm co-founder, the story's anchor in the system
- **Uncle Ern** — former spook, philosopher, the story's anchor outside it
- **Heeni / Sparkles** — spiritual core, likely true protagonist
- **Adrian, Pak-Lim, Mae, Song, Vicki** — supporting cast, each carrying a thread

## The central mechanic (V2 key insight)
**Soul transference between Ern and Heeni.**
- Ern's reflection (young woman, blank face) — it's already happened
- Heeni's near-death on the bike — the hinge point; time dissolves,
  consciousness becomes permeable
- Aunty Tia's death — the catalyst
- Heeni's cold clarity, false memories, sudden facility with physics —
  Ern leaking through
- Ern's "dead person on holidays" — he's known for a while

## Technical / thematic thread
- **WhoComm** — the identity platform
- **secsym (Secrets with Symmetry)** — protocol where identity exists
  only in relationship; the technical metaphor for the philosophical spine
- **@man / Project Atman** — still unresolved; the mystery at the centre
- **Philosophical spine** — bureaucratic/fixed identity vs.
  universal/relational awareness
