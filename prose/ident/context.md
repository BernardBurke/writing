## The project

**Ident** is a speculative fiction manuscript set in the near future.
Central theme: the collision between fixed bureaucratic identity systems
and fluid, relational, universal selfhood.

V1 is complete. V2 is in active planning and drafting.
Repo: `writing/prose/ident/` — markdown format, open-source via git.

## The soul-transference mechanic
The central speculative conceit: something of Ern transfers into — or through —
Heeni (Sparkles). Not a literal ghost. More like: a pattern, a way of seeing,
a relational mode that persists after Ern is gone.

This needs to be technically and emotionally coherent by V2.
The @man / Atman thread is likely the mechanism or the mirror for this.

---

## Technical thread
- **WhoComm** — the identity/communications system (digital identity infrastructure)
- **secsym** — a protocol or symbol system within WhoComm (details TBC from chapters)
- **@man / Atman** — mysterious handle or entity; likely connects to Hindu/Buddhist
  concept of Atman (universal self). May be the philosophical spine made literal.

---

## Philosophical spine
**Fixed bureaucratic identity** (the state sees you as a node, a credential, a record)
vs.
**Universal relational awareness** (you are who you are *in relation* — fluid, contextual,
alive to others)

Ern embodies the second. The system enforces the first.
Heeni inherits the tension.
